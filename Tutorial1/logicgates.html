To the left are the four logic gates that we will make in today's tutorial. Their input fields are on the left and their output fields are on the right. The input fields are editable and will change the state of the gate when you leave the field (in other words change the 1's and 0's and then click the gate to change its state). When a wire is a binary 1 it will be green, and when it is a binary 0 it will be black.
